When I launched The Offer Lab a week ago, I wasn't sure how people would respond.

The support blew me away. So I got back to work. 🛠️

Here's what's new in The Offer Lab:

🧠 SQL Practice Module — 50+ questions to sharpen your technical skills. Write your answer, get instant AI feedback, and actually understand where you went wrong. Built specifically for anyone interviewing for data, analyst, or engineering roles. And if you've seen a question in a real interview that's not in the bank yet — you can submit it for review. This is a community-powered question bank.

📅 Smarter Daily Plan — Your plan now adapts in real time based on what you've done. Complete a task, skip a session, or mark something as practiced — and your suggestions update automatically. No more stale to-do lists.

📊 Weekly Insights — See your progress week over week. Are you studying more or less than last week? Which areas need more attention? Now you'll know.

📈 Richer Progress Tracking — Click on any tag in Progress tab to drill into the details. See exactly what you've done, when you did it, and how it's adding up over time.

🔔 Study Reminders — Get a browser notification to remind you to study. No more losing track of time or letting a day slip by unplanned.

🐛 Bug Fixes — Squashed timezone issues affecting the Daily Plan and Tasks. Your schedule now reflects your local time correctly, no matter where you are.

This is still just the beginning. I'm building this in the open, one feature at a time, based on real feedback from real job seekers.

If you're preparing for interviews right now — or know someone who is — give it a try. It's completely free.

🔗 https://the-offer-lab.vercel.app

And if you've been using it already, I'd love to hear what's working for you and what you'd like to see next. Drop a comment or DM me.
